In order to create a song, you need to craft it, and craft the materials.

Actually, there is three type of craftable resources: instruments, patterns and *songs*

While the right section shows the item being crafted. The left pannel, shows the crafting materials

Each applicable type, has *drop zones* for accepting input resources

drop instrument here

To use the resource on the item being crafted, you need to drag it to the dropzone

Following this principle, you can compose patterns from instruments, and finally, songs from instruments

Also, you can compose your custom instruments, from a range of effects:

  • arpeggiator
  • echo
  • reverb
  • lowpass
  • highpass
  • ... (32 more) ...

Don't worry, if you don't get it at first, you will do with the help of recipes and contextual help

RECIPES

Recipes, are interactive mini-tutorials to explain how to craft something (example: a song)

Some tutorials like 'intro' only explain a few things about the main interface

It's recommendable to follow these recipes if you don't know what to do or how to start using the app. The recipes are reachable from help menu ('?')

CONTEXTUAL HELP

There is a series of tooltips explaining on detail the different options of the app and how to use it. You can enable or disable it by click on the box in left-bottom corner of the screen
